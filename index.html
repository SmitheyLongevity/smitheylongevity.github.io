Privacy & approach

How we work — and how we protect your data.

Smithey Longevity Group helps individuals analyze their own biological data and build personal protocols. We are not a medical practice. The work we do is analytical and educational — interpreting your data and helping you act on it. We do not diagnose conditions, prescribe medications, or provide medical treatment. For all clinical care, we encourage clients to maintain their relationship with a physician.

Any data you share with me — wearable exports, lab results, intake questionnaires, conversation notes — is treated as confidential. It is stored on encrypted infrastructure under our control, under strict access control, and never sold or shared with third parties. You can request deletion of all your data at any time.

If you are in crisis, experiencing a medical emergency, or seeking mental health support, please contact your physician, a licensed mental health professional, or in the U.S., call or text 988.
